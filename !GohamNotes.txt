Sunday School

As Children's Sunday school department , our aim is to lay a foundation of faith by  teaching biblical truths at an early age, and creating a community where kids feel known and loved within the church.

Goham intercessory:
It is a department which deals with standing in the gap for the sake of the church progress and breakthrough.
 The core purpose of intercessors it is to make sure that all things move according to the glory of God through the leading of prayers and helping people fight the battles of life and the ministry in order to be able to fulfill the assignment of our lord Jesus Christ.
 

YOUTH MINISTRY VISION DOCUMENT:

 VISION STATEMENT
To raise a Christ-like generation empowered with divine ability to lead by example in speech, conduct, love, faith, and purity, making a lasting difference in their communities and the world, inspired by 1 Timothy 4:12.
 MISSION STATEMENT
To equip, mentor, and empower young people through the Word of God, discipleship, and practical life training to grow in Christ, discover their purpose, and boldly influence their generation for God.
 CORE VALUES

    Christ-likeness

We strive to reflect the character of Christ in all we do.

    Faith

We build strong  (belief) in God through teaching, prayer, and trust in His Word.

    Integrity (Purity)

We uphold holiness and moral excellence in thoughts, words, and actions.

    Love

We demonstrate genuine care, unity, and compassion toward others.

    Leadership by Example

We encourage young people to lead through their lifestyle, not just words, as taught in 1 Timothy 4:12.

    Service

We actively serve our church, community, and 0beyond.
 OBJECTIVES (What We Aim to Do)
Develop spiritually mature and disciplined youth.
Raise confident leaders who influence others positively
A generation that stands out in faith, character, and purpose.
Motto 
“Raising A Christic Generation With Divine Ability To Make A Difference.”

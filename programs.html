Our Ministries

Departments & Programs

Children's Ministry

Sunday School

10:00 – 11:00 Every Sunday

As our Children's Sunday School department, our aim is to lay a foundation of faith by teaching biblical truths at an early age, and creating a community where kids feel known and loved within the church.

"Train up a child in the way he should go: and when he is old, he will not depart from it."

Proverbs 22:6 — King James Version
Prayer Ministry

Goham Intercessory

Monday · 16:00 – 18:00 Weekly Intercession

The Goham Intercessory Department is called to stand in the gap for the sake of the church's progress and breakthrough. We believe that prayer is the engine room of every spiritual advance.

The core purpose of our intercessors is to ensure that all things move according to the glory of God — through the leading of prayer, helping people fight the battles of life and ministry, so that the full assignment of our Lord Jesus Christ may be fulfilled in this house.

Music & Worship

Worship Team

15:00 – 18:00 Tuesday & Saturday — Rehearsals

We are the Divine Heavenly Worshippers — assigned to preach the gospel, win souls, and glorify His name. Through music, song, and the leading of the Holy Spirit, we create an atmosphere where hearts are turned toward God and lives are transformed in His presence.

Young People's Ministry

Youth Ministry

14:00 – 15:30 Sunday — After Service

Vision: To raise a Christ-like generation empowered with divine ability to lead by example in speech, conduct, love, faith, and purity — making a lasting difference in their communities and the world, inspired by 1 Timothy 4:12.

Mission: To equip, mentor, and empower young people through the Word of God, discipleship, and practical life training — so they may grow in Christ, discover their purpose, and boldly influence their generation for God.

Core Values
Christ-likeness

We strive to reflect the character of Christ in all we do.

Faith

We build strong belief in God through teaching, prayer, and trust in His Word.

Integrity

We uphold holiness and moral excellence in thoughts, words, and actions.

Love

We demonstrate genuine care, unity, and compassion toward others.

Leadership

We encourage young people to lead through their lifestyle, not just words — as taught in 1 Timothy 4:12.

Service

We actively serve our church, our community, and beyond.

Objectives
  • Develop spiritually mature and disciplined youth.
  • Raise confident leaders who influence others positively.
  • Cultivate a generation that stands out in faith, character, and purpose.
Motto

"Raising A Christic Generation With Divine Ability To Make A Difference."

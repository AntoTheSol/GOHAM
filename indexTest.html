About Our Church

Bishop Simon K Christic
Bishop

Serving GOHAM Church since 2016, Pastor Simon leads with a heart for the community and a passion for Biblical teaching.

Founded in 2016, Gates of Heaven Almighty Ministries International (GOHAM) has been a cornerstone of faith and community in our region. What began as a small gathering of believers has grown into a vibrant congregation united by love, service, and the living Word of Jesus Christ.

We believe that every person has worth and dignity, and that Jesus is the hope of the world. Through Him, our doors are open to all.
